Site Name: Bagnes Family Genealogy

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the Bagnes' family ancestry, and the Basque people and culture, in order to increase interest within the family in performing genealogical research, and temple work.

Site Purpose:

Provide images and information about the Bagnes family on the main page, and about each ancestral family: Bagques and Jimenez on the associated pages. The main page will include information on the family's history following their meeting in Mauleon France, as well as information about the Basque nation and culture. The pages for each lineal family: Bagues & Jimenez, will include images of the families origin city and the basic bios of the known family members.

Scenarios:

Where is our family from?
Who are the Basque?
How did they live?

Color Schema:

The primary color used for this site will be #DA1219, taken from the Basque flag. It will be used on the header, h1, and footer elements; along with white text to allow sufficent contrast.
The secondary color used for this site will be #FCDD07, also taken from the Basque flag. It will be used for subheadings: h2, & h3.
Accent colors used will be #E7E7E7, & #CAB312. They will be used to color the background of sections containing images or biographies as appropriate. Another background option for these areas are hero images of the local areas loaded from the associated images folder.
Text colors used for the header and footer will be white. The font color #0F47AF will be used in connect with the Secondary color #FCDD07. And black text will be used for the #E7E7E7, & #CAB312 color backgrounds.

Typography:

Paragraph/Section Font: Radley-regular-italic.
