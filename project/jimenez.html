Petilla de Aragón, Navarra, Spain

Petilla de Aragón is a small municipality in the Navarra region of Northern Spain. It resides within the area known locally as Basque Country. The municipality is formed of two enclaves, Petilla de Aragón, and Los Bastanes and are entirely surrounded by the neighboring autonomous community of Aragón. At the time the jimenez family resided there Petilla de Aragón was a small, hillside community that subsisted largely upon wheat.+
